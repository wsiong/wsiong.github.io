Welcome

Dr Ng obtained his Ph.D in Computer Science from National University of Singapore ( (NUS) in 2004. Currently, he is the Urban Systems Lab Head of Data Analytics Department, the Agency of Science, Technology and Research (A*STAR). He was also the Principal Investigator of the A*STAR Data Analytics Exchange Platform (A*DAX") project, and Co-Director of I2R-LTA and I2R-URA Joint Lab. His areas of expertise include Cloud Computing, Big Data Management, Data Mining and Data Analytics, Data Privacy and Smart Cities Applications and Urban Logistics.
