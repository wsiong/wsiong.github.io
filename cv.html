SUMMARY

Dr Ng obtained his Ph.D in Computer Science from National University of Singapore (NUS) in 2004. Previously, he served as the core system architect of a local IT firm (Singapore), which is specialized in Content Management and Multimedia Processing (i.e., text, images, video and audio), where he held various responsibilities including systems architecting, solutions consulting, project management and sale management.

Currently, he is a Lab Head, Data Management at the Institute for Infocomm Research (I2R), which is a member of the Agency for Science, Technology and Research (A*STAR) family. His areas of expertise are Cloud Computing, Data Management, peer-to-peer data management, databases optimization, distributed and mobile systems.

RESEARCH PHILOSOPHY

Wee Siong’s research philosophy is fairly simple – he believes that research should always be about building and evaluating working prototype systems that address problems encountered by practitioners. His research interests lie on the practical side where the results are there to be seen and can have an immediate impact.

RESEARCH INTERESTS

Wee Siong's major research interests include Cloud computing, Big Data Processing, Smart Cities Applications, Data Privacy and Data Management
